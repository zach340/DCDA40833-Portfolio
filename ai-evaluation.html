AI Tool Evaluation

Introduction

I wanted to explore the coding efficiencies because these machines are becoming more capable of coding large projects quickly and with littlehuman inputs. From my experience they can give good large coding blocks that may need some debugging but are much quicker than the human coders.

Tool 1: Perplexity

Capabilities

Perplexity is good about creating code when given exactly what you want or as much detail as you need. Where it comes short is that when given some basic instructions it may miss key details that you felt like were need it get to the final product like it will sometimes skip a step. It also has limitations on how it will format certain parts and knowing the overall code when given this limited input.

Appropriate Use

Good uses cases are when you know exactly what you want it to create because then you can work with it to get to what you had pictured in your head. A poor use case is if you are trying to get it to build something and you do not have the experience or knowledge that it may need to get to what you desire. If you give it minimal directions it does not know what kind of structures you may need. So, if you have an end goal in mind then it will work well for you, but it may not be the best to start if you do not already have some background for what you are trying to get it to do.

Ethical Considerations

One ethical concern might be what they do with our data. On their website they claim that they do not sell any of your data, but they do track off website activities for better usage of the application. Some data may be used to train AI models and improve the quality of search results, unless you choose to opt out through your Account Settings. So it does collect your input to train but you can opt out of this process.
